Laser Tattoo Removal Aftercare

Instructions

  • Blisters can appear 8 hours after your laser tattoo removal treatment. If blisters are part of your healing process, they are normally held between 4-6 days and scabbing can last up to two weeks
  • Do not pick the scabs, blisters, or crusts that form after your laser tattoo removal treatment. Picking at the skin directly relates to any scarring that may occur
  • Use sunscreen to protect the area treated for at least 3 months after each session
  • Do not apply make-up or anything that may irritate the healing area. Scented products can irritate healing skin
  • Avoid sauna, hot showers & baths for after the treatment until the area has healed
  • If you experience discomfort use an ice pack for 5-10 minutes after the treatment
